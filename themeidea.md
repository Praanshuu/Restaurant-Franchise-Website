* Text: Deep charcoal (not pure black)

Start light → build trust

# 🎨 1. COLOR SYSTEM (Finalized)

## 🧱 Base Palette (Warm Premium)

* **Background (Primary):** `#FAF7F2` → warm off-white (main canvas)
* **Surface (Cards / Sections):** `#F1ECE6` → subtle depth
* **Text Primary:** `#1E1E1E` → soft black (not harsh)
* **Text Secondary:** `#6B6B6B` → muted grey

---

## 🌑 Dark Sections (Strategic Depth)

* **Dark Background:** `#191919`
* **Dark Surface:** `#242424`
* **Text on Dark:** `#F5F5F5`

---

## 🔥 Accent Colors (Controlled, not loud)

* **Primary Accent (CTA):** `#C67C4E` → warm burnt caramel
* **Accent Hover:** `#A9653D`
* **Secondary Accent (optional):** `#D4AF37` → muted gold

👉 Avoid bright red / neon → kills premium feel

---

# 🔤 2. TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM (This defines premium feel)

## Headings (Luxury + Emotion)

👉 Use: **Playfair Display**

* H1: 48–64px
* H2: 32–40px
* Letter spacing: slightly tight
* Line height: ~1.2

---

## Body (Clarity + Modern)

👉 Use: **Inter**

* Body: 16–18px
* Small text: 14px
* Line height: 1.6–1.8

---

## CTA Text

* Slightly bold (500–600 weight)
* Clean and short
* No fancy fonts

---

# 📐 3. SPACING SYSTEM (THIS is what makes it look expensive)

Use an **8px grid system**

### Section spacing:

* Top/Bottom: **80–120px**

### Inside sections:

* Gap: **24–40px**

### Cards:

* Padding: **24px or 32px**

---

👉 Rule:

> If it feels cramped → it’s not premium

---

# 🧱 4. LAYOUT SYSTEM

## Container

* Max width: **1200px**
* Center aligned
* Side padding: **20–24px**

---

## Grid

* Desktop: 2-column / 3-column
* Mobile: single column

---

## Flow

* Alternate sections (light → dark → light)
* Never stack similar backgrounds repeatedly

---

# 🎯 5. COMPONENT DESIGN

## 🔘 Buttons

Primary CTA:

* Background: `#C67C4E`
* Text: white
* Border radius: 8px
* Padding: 12px 24px

Hover:

* Slight darken + subtle scale (1.02)

---

Secondary CTA:

* Outline style
* Border: `#C67C4E`
* Text: same color

---

## 🧾 Cards

* Background: `#F1ECE6`
* Border radius: 12–16px
* Shadow: very soft (almost invisible)

---

## 🧭 Navbar

* Transparent initially
* Becomes solid on scroll
* Sticky

CTA button always visible

---

# 🖼️ 6. IMAGE STYLE GUIDE (VERY IMPORTANT)

Use images that feel:

* Warm lighting
* Real people (not stocky fake smiles)
* Close-up food shots
* Natural textures (wood, plates, steam)

---

Avoid:

* Over-edited HDR images
* Neon lighting
* Pure white backgrounds

---

# ⚡ 7. MICRO-INTERACTIONS

Keep it subtle.

* Smooth scroll
* Fade-in sections (on scroll)
* Button hover scale
* Image slight zoom on hover

👉 No flashy animations

---

# 🧠 8. SECTION UI DIRECTION (Quick Mapping)

## Hero:

* Split layout (text + image)
* Large typography

---

## Experience Section:

* 3 cards (Family / Hangout / Date)
* Each with image + short copy

---

## System Section:

* Horizontal steps or vertical timeline

---

## Franchise Section:

* Dark background
* Grid layout (info + benefits)
